Fruits: Nature's Sweetness

A brief overview of common fruits and their rich nutritional content, including essential vitamins and minerals.

Popular Fruits

  • Apple - Rich in fiber, contains Vitamin C, Potassium.
  • Banana - Great source of Potassium, Vitamin B6, Magnesium.
  • Orange - Packed with Vitamin C, Manganese, Folate.
  • Berries (Strawberries, Blueberries) - Antioxidants, Vitamin C, Manganese.

Fruits & Essential Minerals

Fruits provide various minerals vital for our body's functions:

  • Potassium: Bananas, Oranges, Cantaloupe [8].
  • Magnesium: Avocados, Bananas, Figs [8, 11].
  • Manganese: Berries, Pineapple, Spinach.
  • Calcium (Small Amounts): Oranges, Figs [8].

Minerals for Health

Minerals support bone health, nerve function, and hydration:

  • Iron: Essential for oxygen transport (found in dried apricots).
  • Zinc: Important for immune function (in pumpkin seeds, not fruit).
